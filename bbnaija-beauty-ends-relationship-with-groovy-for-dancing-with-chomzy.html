    Beauty Ends Relationship With Groovy For Dancing With Chomzy

    Beauty during their Saturday night had challenged Groovy for dancing with Chomzy, which eventually led to a serious argument in the house. The party had in attendance housemates from the Level One and Level Two part of the reality show.

    17 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Big Brother Naija housemate, Beauty on Saturday ended her relationship with Groovy for dancing with Chomzy during their party.

    Beauty during their Saturday night had challenged Groovy for dancing with Chomzy, which eventually led to a serious argument in the house.

    The party had in attendance housemates from the Level One and Level Two part of the reality show.

    Beauty during the argument noted that she had earlier warned Groovy not to dance with Chomzy, but he is permitted to dance with every other girl.

    The reality star, however, did not give a reason for warning Groovy not to dance with Chomzy.

    She said, “I told you to dance with every other girl except that girl, but even when I dragged her away from you, you gave her a high-five and she started laughing at me.

    “Who are you? You want to embarrass me. I saw you wrapping your hands around her waist.”

    Groovy in his reaction to her claims said, “she was the one that came to me. I didn’t go to her. She came to me and I didn’t dance with her.”

    Beauty, however, was not ready to listen to an explanation as she ended the relationship, she said, “it’s over. I never want to talk to you again. Keep away from me. Who do you think you are?”
