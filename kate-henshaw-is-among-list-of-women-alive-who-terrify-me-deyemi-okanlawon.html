    15 May 2024, 00:00

    Nollywood actor, Deyemi Okanlawon, has gushed over the beauty of his colleague, Kate Henshaw, while wondering how she keeps looking younger despite her age.

    Naija News reports that the actress cum fitness expert who turned 52 a few days ago is among other top celebrities that are considered to be ‘ageing backwards’.

    Taking to his Instagram page on Friday night, Deyemi shared a photo from the movie ‘Blood Sisters’ in which he starred alongside Kate Henshaw.

    According to him, his colleague is at the top of the list of women alive who terrify him, including a heartfelt concern for people, passion for work and the energy she displays.

    He wrote: “Some of you will see this pic and be doing “Awww she looks so gentle & pretty!” Lemme just tell you ppl now @k8henshaw is at the top of the list of women alive who terrify me! Her heart for people, her passion for her craft, her energy for life is really a terrifying wonder to behold… and dear ancient… I mean big… sis, we celebrate your full 2052 yrs on earth in peace and love”

    Kate Henshaw Shares Three Facts About Herself

    Meanwhile, Kate Henshaw has shared three facts about herself.

    The thespian, who recently turned 52, posted pictures of herself in three different personality types and described herself in three words, fit, fab, and fun.

    The actress, who is known for her dedication to working out, made it clear that she is committed to living a life of holistic wellness for her body, mind, and spirit.

    She referred to herself as an unflappable warrior who would not remain silent.
