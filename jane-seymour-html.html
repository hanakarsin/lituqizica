    28 May 2024, 00:00

    British actress who became known as TV's Dr. Quinn, Medicine Woman and was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress for her portrayal of Wallis Simpson in the 1988 film The Woman He Loved.

    Before Fame

    Her real name is Joyce Penelope Wilhelmina Frankenberg, and she took King Henry VIII's third wife's name as her stagename.

    Trivia

    She was made a member of the Order of the British Empire in 2000.

    Family Life

    She has been married and divorced four times. She welcomed two children with her third husband David Flynn and later welcomed two more children with her fourth husband James Keach.

    Associated With

    She co-starred with Roger Moore in the 1973 James Bond film Live and Let Die.
