    APC 2023 Presidential Aspirants, Billions Made So Far On Forms Sale

    Recall that APC had pegged its presidential forms to the sum of N100million per aspirant. Recall that the party revealed it had made N1.630 billion on Thursday from the sale of its forms after 17 presidential aspirants picked their forms.

    08 May 2024, 00:00

    The ruling All Progressive Congress, APC has so far raked in two billion four hundred thirty million Naira from sale of Expression of Interest and Nomination forms to 25 presidential aspirants, Naija News reports.

    Recall that APC had pegged its presidential forms to the sum of N100million per aspirant.

    Recall that the party revealed it had made N1.630 billion on Thursday from the sale of its forms after 17 presidential aspirants picked their forms.

    There was a ‘shortfall’ of seventy million from the figures reported above due to the fact that the only female presidential aspirant in the list, Aishat Alubankudi, was allowed to pay N30million for the forms, Naija News reports

    See below an updated list of the APC presidential aspirants hoping to succeed President Muhammadu Buhari in the 2023 general elections.

    Meanwhile, the National Organising Secretary of APC, Alhaji Sulaiman Arugugu, had confirmed earlier that a total of 241 aspirants picked the party’s senatorial forms.

    The party chieftain disclosed further that 821 aspirants Picked House of Representatives while no fewer than 1,505 aspirants picked States House of Assembly tickets.
