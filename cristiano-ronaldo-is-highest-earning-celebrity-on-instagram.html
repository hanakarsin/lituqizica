    04 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Al Nassr and Portuguese football icon Cristiano Ronaldo is currently the highest-paid celebrity on Instagram with the highest number of followers.

    In comparison to American socialite Kylie Jenner, 25, who earns £1.47 million for each sponsored post, Cristiano Ronaldo, 38, earns an astounding £1.87 million per sponsored Instagram post, according to Mail Online.

    He has an astounding 597 million followers on the social media platform, with Kylie right behind him with 397 million followers.

    Football player Lionel Messi is third on the most recent Instagram Rich List and he earns £1.38 million per sponsored post.

    Hollywood icon Dwayne Johnson is in 5th place and earns £1.33 million per post. The wrestler turn actor is closely behind 4th placed Selena Gomez, who earns £1.35 million for each post.

    Kim Kardashian is 6th on the Instagram rich list as she earns £1.31 million per sponsored post.

    While 8th placed Beyonce gets £1.08 million from each post, Ariana Grande came in seventh on the list with £1.3 million per post.

    Khloe Kardashian and Kendall Jenner, who earn £1.31 million each from sponsored posts, were ranked 9th and 10th, respectively.

    HopperHQ created the Instagram Rich List based on the maximum price that any user may charge for a single Instagram post.

    The company examines statistics on follower numbers, posting frequency, average engagement, and industry-specific criteria.
