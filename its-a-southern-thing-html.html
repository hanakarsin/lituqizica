    Launched under a division of Alabama Media Group called Red Clay Media, the group produces videos that showcase hilarious moments that come with living in the South. They are the people behind the Facebook Watch show So True, Ya'll.

    Trivia

    They were a 2019 Shorty Award finalist in the category of Facebook Presence. Their Facebook account has earned more than 1.3 million followers.

    Members

    Members of the It's a Southern Thing team included Matt Mitchell, Haley Laurence, Adam Schwartz, Lauren Musgrove, Luke Porter, Justin Yurkanin, Elizabeth Whitmire, Talia Lin, Kelly Kazek and Jared Boyd.
