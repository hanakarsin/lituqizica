    Pastor Liz Muyu and her husband Ocampo are not your regular lovebirds. They did not love each other from the onset.

    The two got engaged without consent during a family vacation but ended up loving each other fiercely after walking down the aisle.

    For a few months, the pastor's mum urged her to settle down with Ocampo but she was not open to any arranged shenanigans because she regarded herself a modern lady.

    During a family vacay in the Philippines, Ocampo's father went on the pulpit and told his church members that finally his son had found a woman he wanted to marry.

    Liz was against the whole arrangement and at some point, she blocked Ocampo on social media.
