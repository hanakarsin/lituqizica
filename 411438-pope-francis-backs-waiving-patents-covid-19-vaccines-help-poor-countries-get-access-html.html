    US backs patents waiver

    Pope's plea comes after the United States Government endorsed a move at the World Trade Organisation (WTO) seeking to temporarily waive intellectual property protections for COVID-19 jabs.

    In a statement on Wednesday, May 5, US Trade Representative Katherine Tai cautioned that the process was likely to take some time due to the complexity of the issues involved.

    She further observed that Joe Biden's administration believed strongly in intellectual property protections.

    "I also want to make it clear that as much as we are going to take part in these negotiations at the WTO, let it be on record that the outcome will take time due to the issues around it," she explained.
