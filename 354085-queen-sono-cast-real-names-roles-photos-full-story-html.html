    Written by South African filmmaker Kagiso Lediga, the six-episode drama series Queen Sono premiered on Netflix on February 28, 2020. It is the first-ever African original series from Netflix, and surprisingly, it has drawn lots of attention with its amazing and captivating plot.

    Wondering what characters made up the Queen Sono cast? It was a star-studded series with lots of talented African actors.

    Queen Sono synopsis

    Queen Sono is a crime drama television series that centres on the main character with the same name. Queen Sono is a highly trained undercover secret agent who has to navigate the world of criminals in politics and business to uncover the truth about Sayina Sono, her mother’s death.

    As a member of the Special Operations Group (SOP) which is a South African Intelligence Unit, Queen is often tasked with various dangerous missions around the African continent that deal with corruption, European neocolonialism, and terrorism. However, the suspicions behind her mother’s assassination make her question the loyalty of those closest to her.

    Queen Sono - Pearl Thusi

    Queen Sono was described as an elite spy in a South African agency. Queen Sono was portrayed by Pearl Thusi, who was once a beauty queen and now a celebrated actress. She has appeared in movies and television series such as Quantico, The No.1 Ladies Detective Agency, and Isidingo.

    Shandu Johson Magwaza - Vuyo Dabula

    Shandu was Queen Sono's ex-boyfriend and colleague at work. However, he ditched his work to become the leader of a dangerous rebellious group called Watu Wema.

    The person behind this character was Vuyo Dabula, who has had an illustrious career as an actor. Among the notable films that he has appeared are Mandela: Long Walk to Freedom, Invictus, and Avengers: Age of Ultron.

    Dr. Sidwell Isaacs - Sechaba Morojele

    Sechaba Morojele portrayed Dr. Sidwell Isaacs, the chief director of the Special Operations Group (SOG). In the series, he portrayed a no-nonsense leader who occasionally relied on Queen to execute highly risky missions.

    Frequently asked questions

    Here are answers to some of the common questions asked about the Queen Sono series.

    Is there a season 2 of Queen Sono?

    The season one of the Netflix series Queen Sono is having quite a warm reception among movie fanatics. Interestingly, Netflix has revealed that Queen Sono season 2 is in the offing and might be out soon.

    What language are they speaking in Queen Sono?

    English is the predominantly used language in the series. However, the characters also speak different native languages in Africa, such as Swahili, Zulu, and Afrikaans.

    Where is Queen Sono filmed?

    It was shot in approximately 37 different scenes in Johannesburg, which include the central business district, Soweto, and Park Station. Outside South Africa, it was captured in countries such as Kenya, Nigeria, Tanzania, and Zanzibar.
