    Nigerians Tackle Reno Omokri For 'Professing Love' To Actress, Omotola Jalade (Video)

    Naija News reports that Reno and Omotola were part of the guests who stormed Senator Ben Murray-Bruces daughters wedding in the U.S over the weekend. A video shared on his Facebook page captured the controversial political commentator gushing over Omotola as he confessed love to her.

    17 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Former presidential aide, Reno Omokri, has been attacked online for professing love to veteran Nollywood actress, Omotola Jalade-Ekeinde.

    Naija News reports that Reno and Omotola were part of the guests who stormed Senator Ben Murray-Bruce’s daughter’s wedding in the U.S over the weekend.

    A video shared on his Facebook page captured the controversial political commentator gushing over Omotola as he ‘confessed love’ to her.

    Reno was also, however, quick to correct an impression saying he was confessing agape love to the actress as he happily side-hugged the married Nollywood star.

    However, the video raised a number of interesting reactions from many Nigerians with claims that Reno was touching Omotola inappropriately as she seemed to have expressed unease in the video.

    E for Eagle wrote: “With her facial expression and body language you could tell she abhors you sir.. stop forcing yourself on her, beside she’s a married woman”

    Ezeorji Arthur Nnaemeka wrote: “Oga Reno, please know that Omotola is happily married. Please don’t mistake her to that Ethiopian lady”

    Joana Chidimma Ezemson wrote: “She doesn’t like the way you held her, see her feeling uncomfortable and forming laugh. Abi na mouth odor de push her away”

    Ukandu Promise wrote: “You’re now toasting another lady, covering up as agape Love… Na so the Ethiopian woman own take start”

    Gorzie Ishiocha Nweke wrote: “You too like woman!!! See the kind smile wey full your face. No allow Ethiopian woman see this video, if not she don throw your things out of her house.”

    Maurice Osama Ogbebor wrote: “The lady was definitely confused… You made a video to tell her you love her agapely… This is bizarre, I’m as confused as omotola, The worst part is you post the video, what for? Validation?”

    Immaculata Etim wrote: “Daddy when will you post with your wife like this???? Abi na pesin wife dey sweet u to dey do Agape love with”

    Somto Chinanu wrote: “Even omotola is not happy with you…….. The smile isn’t from her heart”
