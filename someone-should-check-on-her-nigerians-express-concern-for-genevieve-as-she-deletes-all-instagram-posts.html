    'Someone Should Check On Her'

    21 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Some Nigerians have expressed concern for veteran Nollywood actress Genevieve Nnaji as she deletes all her posts on the photo and video sharing app, Instagram.

    This comes a few days after Genevieve Nnaji celebrated her 43rd birthday without sharing any post on her social media pages.

    However, Genevieve Nnaji’s unusual absence on social media has raised concerns from some Nigerians worried about what could be wrong with the actress.

    Genevieve returned to Instagram a few hours ago and deleted all her posts and pictures, leaving netizens questioning the reason for the sudden removal of all her posts.

    While some Nigerians took time to pray for Genevieve Nnaji others called on her close friends and colleagues to check up on her.

    Although when some Nigerian celebrities deleted all their Instagram post, it is usually speculated that such people want to start a new life.

    ebubechukwu_love wrote: “Someone should check on her pls. She might be depressed”

    stylishimperialdesigns wrote: “Not everyone appreciates all these fan pressures seriously I do feel for those ppl that can’t be themselves anymore because of the fans. Imagine when people think u don’t get old, u can’t have wrinkles, u can’t be imperfect Hana! E reach to leave social media abeg”.

    mo_fabricks wrote: “We hope she’s fine . Our love and thoughts are with her”.

    shirley_ete wrote: “I hope someone close to her is doing a welfare check, because we never know what someone is going through privately. Praying for her”.
