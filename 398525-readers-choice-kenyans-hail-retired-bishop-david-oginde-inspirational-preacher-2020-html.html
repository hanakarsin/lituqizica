    Readers choice: Kenyans hail retired bishop David Oginde as most inspirational preacher in 2020

    - A TUKO.co.ke poll sought to seek the views of Kenyans concerning the most inspirational preacher of 2020 - Most mentioned David Oginde as a true man of God whose knowledge in the word of God is incredible

    20 Apr 2024, 00:00

    - A TUKO.co.ke poll sought to seek the views of Kenyans concerning the most inspirational preacher of 2020

    - Most mentioned David Oginde as a true man of God whose knowledge in the word of God is incredible

    - The respondents also hailed the other preachers in the poll namely Cathy Kiuna, Lucy Natasha and Pastor T Mwangi

    - They said each of the preachers had played their roles in nourishing Kenyans spiritually

    Kenyans have hailed retired CITAM church bishop David Oginde as the most inspirational preacher in 2020.

    Many Kenyans cited the bishop's deep knowledge of the word of God and inspirational sermons that always left them spiritually nourished in 2020.

    The TUKO.co.ke Facebook poll pitting Oginde, Lucy Natasha, Pastor T and Cathy Kiuna, revealed many Kenyans had much respect for the Man of God with many saying he had surpassed all expectations.

    At the same time, Kenyans also had kind words for the other three preachers saying they each played a significant role in 2020 on matters of spirituality.

    Catherine K Mghana said:

    "Yes, Bishop Oginde is my retired church bishop. I have all the respect and honour for his leadership, but when it comes to spiritual leaders I salute them all".

    Catherine Kamau wrote:

    "Bishop David Oginde exceeded expectation by far. Make a point of listening to his sermons and you'll agree with me".

    Ole Njoka said

    Bishop Oginde; his leadership skills articulated on the job, and through the weekly Leadership programme on Hope FM, is an absolute game-changer in Christian ministry".

    Fred Ogada said all of the named servants of God were fantastic in their own but singled out Pastor T Mwangi as an inspiration to the youths.

    On her part, Mariam Namutosi said she admired all the men and women of God but she also adored Natasha especially due to her philanthropic work.

    TUKO.co.ke's top fan Eliza Munya said Cathy Kiuna was a special preacher because of her message to all the marriages that had lost hope in 2020.

    "She's so passionate about marriage and making it work and given that we are living in a world where marriage has lost its meaning, she's doing an excellent job in giving hope," she said.
