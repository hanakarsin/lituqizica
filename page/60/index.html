President Buhari Extends Tenure Of IGP Mohammed Adamu

17 Jun 2024, 00:00

President Muhammadu Buhari has extended the tenure of the Inspector General of Police, Mohammed Adamu by three months. This development was disclosed on Thursday afternoon by the Minister of Police Affairs, Maigari Dingyadi while briefing State House correspondents at the Presidential Villa, Abuja. He noted that the extension of the IGP’s tenure by the president is to allow enough time to select a new police boss. Naija News reports that Adamu was expected to leave the office on Monday 1st February 2021, having completed his 35 years maximum stay in service.

Supreme Court Rules In Favour Of Abba Kabir-Yusuf

17 Jun 2024, 00:00

The Supreme Court has confirmed Engineer Abba Kabir-Yusuf as the authentic governorship candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) in Kano state. The Justice Bode Rhodes-Vivour led panel dismissed the appeal of an aspirant for the PDP’s governorship ticket, Ibrahim Ali-Amin. The Supreme Court affirmed the decision of the Court of Appeal in Kaduna which held that Yusuf was not joined as a party in the suit by Ali-Amin. Naija News reports that Ali-Amin had challenged the nomination of Kabir-Yusuf at a Federal High Court in Kano.
