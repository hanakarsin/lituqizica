Bandit Terrorists Abduct Nigerian Soldiers In Borno

Bandit terrorists have reportedly kidnapped two Nigerian Army personnel on Maiduguri-Damaturu highway, Goni Masari area in Borno. Their kidnap was disclosed in a wireless message sent by 29 Task Force Brigade and seen by Peoples Gazette.
