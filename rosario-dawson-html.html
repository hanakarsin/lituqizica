    Actress who played roles in Rent, Sin City, and Seven Pounds. Her other credits include 25th Hour, Eagle Eye, Trance, Clerks II, Death Proof, Unstoppable and Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief. In 2015, she began playing Claire Temple on the Netflix series Daredevil.  

    Before Fame

    She made an appearance on Sesame Street as a child. 

    Trivia

    At the age of 15, she was cast in the controversial 1995 film Kids, and her performance in the film helped kickstart her career. 

    Family Life

    She dated Jason Lewis from 2004 to 2006. She also dated director Danny Boyle. She adopted a 12-year-old daughter in 2014. She dated politician Cory Booker from 2020-2022. She began dating poet Nnamdi Okafor in 2022.

    Associated With

    She starred with Will Smith in Men in Black II.
